# Release 2026-06-15

## Tournament hub: groups, standings, scorers & team pages
- The **Tournament** tab is now a real tournament overview instead of just a team list. Three views:
  - **Groups** — full group standings A–L (position, P/W/D/L, goal difference, points), plus the
    combined **Group Stage** table that ranks the best third-placed teams (8 advance).
  - **Scorers** — the tournament top-scorers chart (goals, assists).
  - **Teams** — the original searchable team grid (kept as a browse/fallback view).
- Teams **advancing to the knockout phase are tinted green** in the standings (driven by the official
  "Round of 32" marker, so it covers each group's top two *and* the 8 best third-placed teams).
- Tapping a team opens a **rebuilt team page** with three tabs: **Overview** (stats — played, W-D-L,
  goals for/against, clean sheets, failed-to-score, penalties, cards, formation, recent form), **Matches**
  (the team's fixtures with results / live state), and **Squad** (the roster).
- Standings, top scorers and team stats sync automatically on app startup and after every match finishes.

## Live & finished matches: tabbed detail with a graphical lineup
- The match detail screen now has **three tabs**: **Events** (the goals/cards/subs/VAR timeline),
  **Lineups**, and **My Pick** (your prediction + scoring).
- **Lineups are now shown on a pitch graphically** (like the prediction screen), with a home/away
  switch, the bench below the XI, and per-player markers: ⚽ goals, 🟨/🟥 cards, 🔻 subbed off,
  🔺 subbed on.

## Predictions: pick substitutes + see substitutions
- When the official XI is out, you can now **tap substitutes too** (a SUBS strip below the pitch) to
  predict their goals/cards — previously only the starting XI was selectable.
- The locked match view now shows a **substitutions** list (🟢 on / 🔴 off).

## Matches & Picks: better sorting and "load more"
- **Today** now reads like the Finished tab: **live matches on top**, then the most-recently-played
  first and the day's earliest at the bottom.
- **Finished matches** (and the finished section of your Picks) now load the **3 most recent** with a
  **Load 3 more** button — so opening a group no longer pulls the entire season's history at once.

## Group owners can remove members
- A group **owner** can now remove a member from the Members tab (✕ next to each non-owner). Their
  predictions and points in that group are deleted. Handy when someone forgets their password and makes
  a new account.

## Admin: reset passwords, manage live matches
- Admins can **set a new password** for any user from the admin user page (tell them the new password
  directly — this is a simple reset, not a secure self-serve flow yet).
- The admin **Match Events & Lineups** panel now lists **live matches too** (shown first, with a pulsing
  status), so you can force an events/lineups sync on a match that's in progress — not just finished ones.

## Technical
- **New entities/tables** (migrations `AddStandingsAndTeamStatistics`, `AddTopScorers`,
  `StandingsCompositeKey`; auto-applied on startup):
  - `standings` — one row per (group, team). Unique index is **(group_name, team_id)**, not team, because
    a third-placed team appears in both its group table and the aggregate "Group Stage" best-thirds
    ranking; keying by team alone collapsed it to one row and dropped the team from its group.
  - `team_statistics` — curated slice of `/teams/statistics` (form, W/D/L, goals F/A, clean sheets,
    failed-to-score, penalties, cards summed across intervals, most-played formation).
  - `top_scorers` — denormalised chart rows (no FK to Player/Team; the feed references ids we don't store).
- **API client** (`IApiFootballClient`): `GetStandingsAsync` (parses the nested group→rows shape),
  `GetTeamStatisticsAsync` (object-shaped `response`, tolerant of empty `goals` blocks), `GetTopScorersAsync`
  (rank = response order). New models in `ApiFootballModels.cs`.
- **Sync use cases** (`UseCases/Tournament`): `SyncStandings` (rebuilds the table — delete + insert — so
  multi-table teams are preserved, FK-guarded against unknown teams), `SyncTopScorers` (rebuilds ~20 rows),
  `SyncTeamStatistics(teamId)` (upsert one team). Wired into `StartupSyncService` (standings + scorers) and
  `MatchSchedulerService` after finalize (standings + scorers + the two teams' stats). Team stats also
  refresh lazily from the team-detail endpoint when missing or >30 min old.
- **`TournamentController`**: `GET /api/tournament/standings` (grouped, rank-ordered),
  `GET /api/tournament/topscorers`, `GET /api/tournament/teams/{id}` (`TeamDetailDto`: summary, country,
  standing — real group preferred over "Group Stage", stats, fixtures). New `TournamentDto.cs`;
  `StandingRowDto` carries `GroupName`. Frontend `api/tournament.ts`, types, `TournamentPage` (3-view
  toggle), rebuilt `TournamentTeamPage` (Overview/Matches/Squad), standings/scorers/stat-card styles.
- **Server-side pagination** of finished history. `GetMatches.ExecuteAsync(userId, groupId, finishedTake?)`
  → `PagedMatchesDto { Matches, FinishedTotal }`: all active (live + upcoming) matches always returned,
  finished ordered desc and capped at `finishedTake`. `GetMyPredictions.ExecutePagedAsync(..., finishedTake)`
  → `PagedMyPredictionsDto { Items, FinishedTotal, TotalPicks, TotalPoints, ExactCount }` (aggregate stats
  computed over *all* picks via cheap count/sum queries so the summary stays correct). `BaseQuery` extracted;
  `/predictions/mine` takes `finishedTake` (null = full list for back-compat); `/predictions/user/{id}`
  unchanged. Endpoints: `GET /api/matches?...&finishedTake=N`. Frontend `MatchesTab`/`PicksTab` fetch with a
  `finishedLimit` (starts at 3) and bump it on "Load more"; `.load-more-btn` style.
- **Match detail tabs**: `MatchLivePage` split into `events`/`lineups`/`mypred` tabs (`.match-tabs`).
  `PredictionPitch` gained an optional `bench` prop (tappable SUBS strip); reused read-only for the lineup
  tab with a `badgesFor` that maps goals/cards/sub-in/out. `MatchPredictPage` passes `benchFor(teamId)` and
  added a substitutions timeline to the locked view.
- **Group owner kick**: `KickGroupMember` use case (owner-authorised; refuses owner/self; deletes the
  member's predictions/scores/membership) + `DELETE /api/groups/{id}/members/{userId}`. Frontend
  `kickGroupMember`, MembersTab ✕ button (`.member-kick-btn`).
- **Admin password reset**: `User.SetPasswordHash`; `POST /api/admin/users/{id}/password` (min 6 chars,
  hashed via `IPasswordHasher`). Frontend `setUserPassword` + AdminUserDetail "Reset Password" section.
- **Admin live-match sync**: `AdminController.Matches` now returns live + finished API-Football matches
  (live first, `IsLive`/`ElapsedMinutes` flags); `AdminSync` flags live rows. `sync-events`/`sync-lineups`
  already worked on any real match.
